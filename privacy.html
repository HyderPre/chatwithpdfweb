Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 24/02/2025

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • We collect personal details such as your name, email address, and profile picture, which are obtained when you authenticate through Google OAuth or GitHub login, allowing us to offer a personalized and secure user experience.
  • We store authentication tokens for login verification, ensuring that your account remains secure and protected against unauthorized access every time you log in.
  • For user support inquiries, we may request your contact details, which help us efficiently address your concerns and provide timely assistance.

1.2 Usage & Technical Data

  • We track the type and version of your browser to ensure that our website is compatible with the software you're using, improving both functionality and performance during your visits.
  • Your IP address is recorded for security purposes, primarily to protect our website from malicious activities, such as brute force attacks or other attempts to exploit our system.
  • We gather device information, including operating system and screen resolution, to optimize our website’s display and responsiveness, offering you a seamless user experience across different devices.
  • We analyze your website interactions, including pages visited and time spent on each page, to understand user behavior, which enables us to enhance the site’s content and improve your overall browsing experience.

1.3 Uploaded Files & Chats

  • When you upload PDF files for document analysis, we retain these files for processing and analysis, allowing us to provide accurate results based on your document's content.
  • All chat conversations with the AI are stored to enable us to provide a more effective and personalized experience by retaining context from previous interactions.
  • If you have enabled chat history, we store these conversations to ensure that your preferences and past queries are always accessible for quick reference and continued support.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • We utilize your data to provide you with our services, including enabling the functionality of chat-based PDF analysis, which allows us to extract valuable insights from your documents based on the content you upload.
  • We use Google or GitHub login authentication to securely verify your identity each time you access your account, ensuring your account's safety by minimizing the risk of unauthorized access.
  • We retain your chat history to offer you a more convenient and personalized experience, allowing us to refer to previous interactions and provide better customer support tailored to your needs.
  • We prioritize your security by using your data for fraud prevention and website protection, which includes detecting and mitigating any suspicious activities or potential breaches that could harm your account or the platform.
  • We analyze your usage data to enhance the accuracy and performance of our AI system, continually improving its ability to respond to your queries more effectively and intuitively.
  • We use your information to assist with support inquiries, ensuring that when you reach out, we can respond promptly with the most relevant and helpful solutions based on your account details.

3. Data Security & Protection

  • We guarantee that your personal data is never sold or shared with unauthorized third parties, maintaining strict confidentiality to protect your privacy at all times.
  • OAuth authentication is safeguarded by industry-standard encryption protocols, ensuring that your login credentials are transmitted securely and are protected from unauthorized access during every session.
  • Uploaded PDFs and chat logs are securely stored in encrypted formats, which helps us maintain the integrity of your data while minimizing the risk of potential data breaches or unauthorized retrieval.
  • Our website employs SSL encryption, firewalls, and continuous security updates to safeguard your information from external threats, providing a secure environment for your online interactions and transactions.
