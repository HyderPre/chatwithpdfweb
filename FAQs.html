Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is Veda PDF?

Veda PDF is a chatbot that allows users to upload PDF documents and interact with the content by asking questions and chatting with the bot.

How do I upload a PDF?

To upload a PDF, go to the upload section of the chatbot interface and select the PDF file you want to upload from your device.

Can I ask questions about any PDF?

Yes, you can ask questions about the content of any PDF you upload, and the chatbot will provide answers based on the information in the document.
